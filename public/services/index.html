Frequently Asked Questions

Here's a few of your questions already answered.

How long does it take to build a website?

The timeline for building a website can range anywhere from a few days to a few weeks. Since I specialize in crafting simple, yet effective websites, I typically aim to complete projects within 3 weeks from start to finish. However, this can vary depending on the complexity of your project and my workload at the time. During our initial consultation, I'll provide a more accurate estimate based on your specific needs.

What's the difference between WordPress and a Static HTML/CSS website?

WordPress is a content management system (CMS) that allows users to easily create, manage, and update content without needing to know how to code. It's a powerful and flexible platform with a vast ecosystem of plugins and themes, making it ideal for dynamic websites that require regular updates.

On the other hand, a static HTML/CSS website is built using just HTML, CSS, and possibly some JavaScript, without the need for a database. This type of website is faster, more secure, and requires less maintenance because it doesn't have the same level of complexity as a CMS. Static websites are a great choice for those who don't need frequent content updates and prioritize performance.

How much will my website cost?

During our no-obligation call, I'll discuss your specific requirements and provide you with an hourly rate along with an approximate number of hours I anticipate your project will take. This way, you'll have a clear understanding of the potential costs involved before we proceed.

Can I update the content on my website myself?

Yes, absolutely! If we build your website on WordPress, you'll have access to an easy-to-use admin interface where you can update content, add new pages, or make changes as needed, all without touching the code. If you choose a static HTML/CSS website, content updates will typically require coding knowledge, but I'm always here to assist with any changes you need.

Will my website be mobile-friendly?

Of course! In 2024, it's essential for every website to use a mobile-first approach to stay competitive in both performance and user experience. I've been building websites with this approach for a long time, so you can be confident that your site will look and perform well on any device. Unlike some online tools that create separate mobile versions of your website (which often require more maintenance and code), I'll build a single, responsive design that adapts seamlessly to all screen sizes.

How do you ensure my website will be fast and perform well?

I use a combination of clean, efficient code, optimized images and media, and thoughtful design choices to ensure your website runs smoothly. Additionally, I leverage a variety of professional online performance optimization tools to test and fine-tune your site, ensuring it delivers a fast and seamless user experience.

What's included in your ongoing support?

After your website is launched, I'm here to help with any content or design tweaks, troubleshooting, and other adjustments you might need. Whether it's minor updates, resolving issues, or simply providing guidance, you can rely on me for ongoing support to keep your website in peak condition.

Do you offer SEO services?

I focus on writing SEO-friendly code that both search engines and accessibility tools love. This includes using semantic HTML, optimizing site structure, and ensuring fast load times, which are all crucial for good on-site SEO. However, I do not provide off-site SEO services like link building or content marketing. My goal is to build a solid technical foundation for your website's SEO success.

Can you help with an existing website, or do you only build new ones?

Yes, I can definitely help with an existing website. Whether you need design and UX enhancements, performance optimization, or other updates, I'm here to improve your current site and make it better than ever.

Will my website be secure?

Absolutely. All websites I build will be secured with an SSL certificate to ensure that data transferred between the user and the website is encrypted. For WordPress sites, I also set up security measures like firewall plugins and regular updates to safeguard your site from potential online threats.
