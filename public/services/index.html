Services

Building a website should feel empowering, not overwhelming. I'm here to take care of all the details, whether you need a brand-new site or improvements to your existing one, ensuring the process is smooth and stress-free from start to finish.

Creating Your Brand New Website

Don't have a website yet? Not for long, I'm here to help you!

WordPress Website

Overview: Tailored for those who want the flexibility of managing their own content, WordPress is a powerful and popular choice. With my twelve years of experience, I craft custom WordPress themes that are lightweight, fast, and easy to manage.

Ideal For: Creatives or small businesses who need a dynamic, regularly updated website with a user-friendly admin interface.

Features:

  • Custom-built themes for optimal performance
  • Scalable and easy to update
  • Secure and SEO-friendly

Static HTML/CSS Website

Overview: For clients who want a feather weight, blazing fast, small site that doesn't require frequent updates, I offer custom-built static websites using HTML, CSS, and Eleventy. This approach provides superior performance and minimal maintenance.

Ideal For: Clients who need a small site for displaying information, like an online business card, that prioritizes speed and simplicity.

Features:

  • Blazing fast load times
  • Low maintenance, low upkeep costs
  • Easy to extend and update as needed
