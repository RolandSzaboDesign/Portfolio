Project Description

Péter is a Hungarian-American contemporary fine art painter from Transylvania. He works primarily with oils and creates his own canvas for each of his art pieces as part of the process.

The website, other than the standard informational pages, and a custom gallery, features a conventional blog with newsletter functionality. It will feature a Woocommerce webshop in the near future, once he finalizes his artworks for sale.

We intentionally went for a super minimalistic design for Peter's website, as this was the direction we both wanted to aim for.
